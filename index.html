Welcome to the personal website of James LePage. I am a digital founder, developer, designer, and generalist. Currently, I'm leading the Applied AI Team at Automattic, the makers of WordPress.com, WooCommerce, Tumblr, and more.

Before this, I cofounded several venture-backed companies and experimented online until something worked. WPAI, acquired by Automattic, was my most recent venture. Founded in 2022, it focused on building AI solutions for WordPress1 WordPress is the Content Management System (CMS) that powers nearly 45% of all websites on the internet , including one of the first autonomous AI agents for the web, an AI IDE focused on WordPress, and a ChatGPT alternative... for WordPress.

Before that, I was the technical co-founder of ShareClub, a venture-backed shareholder rewards platform. It facilitated the distribution of in-kind rewards from public companies to individual shareholders. For example, if you invested in Acme Inc and held for 1 month, you could claim a $100 gift card2 This is a heavy oversimplification, but good to conceptualize every year from that company.

Prior to ShareClub, I studied entrepreneurship and AI at Syracuse University, and ran Isotropic, a digital agency, for 4+ years. At Isotropic, we built large ecommerce sites (our largest client had $28M+ ARR in D2C wellness product subscriptions), digital magazines, and critical marketing sites for public companies.
